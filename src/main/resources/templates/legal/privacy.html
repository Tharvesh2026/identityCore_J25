1. What We Collect

When you create an account or sign in, we collect:

  • Email address — either entered directly, or provided by Google/GitHub during OAuth2/OIDC login.
  • Display name — from your account or your identity provider's profile.
  • Authentication method — whether you signed in with a password or via Google/GitHub.
  • Role and permission assignments, for access control purposes.
  • Application audit logs — timestamps, actions taken, and a correlation ID for each request, for security and troubleshooting.

We do not request or store your Google/GitHub password — authentication with those providers happens entirely on their servers; we only receive a token confirming who you are.

2. Cookies We Use

We use only the cookies strictly necessary to operate the Service. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.

CookiePurposeLifespan
JSESSIONIDKeeps you signed in for your current browser sessionUntil browser closes
ICORE_REMEMBER_MEKeeps you signed in across visits, only if you check "Remember Me"Up to 14 days
XSRF-TOKENProtects you from cross-site request forgery attacksSession

Because these cookies are strictly necessary for the Service to function (you cannot stay logged in without them), we don't present a cookie "accept/reject" gate for them — this is consistent with GDPR's ePrivacy guidance, which exempts strictly-necessary cookies from consent requirements. If we ever introduce optional cookies (e.g. analytics), we will ask for your consent separately before setting them.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To enforce role-based access control across the application.
  • To maintain audit logs for security investigation and troubleshooting.
  • To communicate with you about your account, if necessary.

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties except the identity providers you choose to authenticate with (Google, GitHub).

4. Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Audit log entries are retained for a limited period for security purposes and are periodically purged. Remember-me tokens are deleted when you log out, when they expire, or when revoked.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing (where applicable).

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
