We use cookies and similar tracking mechanisms on our application to maintain secure sessions and optimize your user experience.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser on your device. They allow our server to identify your active session and persist your credentials parameters across different navigation clicks.

2. Cookies We Utilize

Cookie Name Type Purpose
JSESSIONID Strictly Necessary Maintains your active authenticated session on the application server. Expires on browser close.
XSRF-TOKEN Security Protects forms and POST requests against Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) attacks.
ICORE_REMEMBER_ME Authentication Persistence Persists your login state for up to 14 days, allowing you to return to the system without re-entering credentials.

3. Controlling Cookies

You can block, disable, or delete cookies via your browser configurations. Note that disabling cookies will cause session-based features and persistent logins to stop functioning.
